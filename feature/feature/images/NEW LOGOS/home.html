About Jay Sands

For over four decades, I’ve had the privilege of sharing timeless music with audiences across the East Coast and beyond. My passion lies in bringing the golden age of song to life, whether I’m channeling Sinatra’s swing or delivering the romantic warmth of Engelbert, Paul Anka, Dean Martin, or The Duprees, to mention a few.

I’ve performed in venues ranging from intimate cabarets to grand stages, from Don't Tell Mama's in New York City to The Ormond Beach Performing Arts Center in Ormond Beach Florida, and Universal’s Portofino Bay Hotel in Orlando, Florida. Every show is a chance to connect, to move people, and to celebrate music that never goes out of style.

When asked to give a quote about me, Don K. Reed of New York’s 101.1 FM said, “Jay Sands not only swings on stage but on his CD as well.” That kind of praise means a lot, because I put my heart into every note I sing.

Whether it’s a private celebration, a corporate event, a public concert, or an online performance, I tailor each show to fit the moment. I’m here to make your event unforgettable, with music that speaks to the soul and leaves a lasting impression.
